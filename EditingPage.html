Editing Experience  

Through a combination of on-campus literary publications and valuable classes, I have acquired a variety of editing
  • Substantive Editing
    In fall 2023, I worked as a substantive editor for Rose Red, one of the novellas chosen for publishing that semester. I worked with a team of editors to collaboratively create and write a feedback letter for the author. This letter focuses on matters of craft, such as pacing, plotting, characterization, themes, and sensitivity.
  • Copyediting
    I have taken a semester-long comprehensive course on copyediting, which has left me proficient in both Chicago Manual of Style and AP Stylebook. In this class, I have copy edited and proofread novel excerpts as well as articles. Furthermore, I was on the copyediting team for another of Wilde Press's publications this semester, TwoSpanTime.
    • Concrete Literary Magazine
    Since fall of 2022, I have been a prose reader for Concrete Literary Magazine on campus, one of Emerson's oldest literary publications. The readers meet once a week to discuss submissions and suggest potential edits for a feedback letter, then vote on acceptance into the magazine.
